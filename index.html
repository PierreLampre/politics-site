Intro to Election Season 2024

Read to get an idea of the upcoming elections here in Cumberland County.

Hey Cumberland County! With the elections coming up and early voting starting in a few weeks, we have a bunch of election related info here on the site. Oftentimes many of us get the sample ballot in the mail or walk into the voting booth not knowing many of the names on the ballot, so hopefully this will help you vote with some more confidence. This year we have 2 seats open on the county commissioner's board, the county clerk seat is open, many school board seats are open, a seat on the Millville city commissioner's board, the Vineland Mayor seat, the US House of Representatives seat, the US Senate seat, the Presidential election, and more. The focus of the site is local elections, as they aren't covered as much in the news. However, we do cover the national elections too, as many people are unaware of 3rd party candidates ahead of elections. Just don't expect any in-depth reviews for races like Trump vs Harris, as that is already widely covered. In fact, don't expect any super in-depth coverage here, as this is a grassroots effort. Think of this site as 'something is better than nothing' since the local newspaper doesn't do this work anymore. You can also view this as a starting point to getting more involved, as we have our suggestions on the Get-Involved section, and we have many local events listed on our Events section... that way you can get started on doing your own research, and forming your own conclusions. If you feel a bit confused or overwhelmed, check out our tutorial video below to get some ideas on how to navigate the site. Also, if you have some objective information, interesting perspectives on the races, or general critiques of the site, you can visit our facebook group. Check back here in a few weeks or a month for updates, as the elections develop over time and the candidates continue to contribute information. Until next time.

VOTE WITH CONFIDENCE

At Inform the Vote, our mission is to establish a new standard of civic engagement by equipping you—the citizen—with thoroughly researched, unbiased, and easily digestible information.

We believe an empowered citizenry is the cornerstone of a transparent and accountable local government. Yet, in an era where this should be the norm, both transparency and accountability often fall short. We're here to challenge this status quo.

Our platform is designed to streamline and consolidate the essential information you need to become an informed voter, breaking down the barriers that often deter civic participation in a process that can and should be more straightforward.
