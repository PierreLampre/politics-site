Inform The Vote

Clear, up to date information on candidates running for election relevant to Cumberland County NJ citizens and where they stand on the issues at hand.

All information gathered by independent citizens of Cumberland County.

Mission Statement

"At Inform the Vote, our mission is to catalyze a new standard of civic engagement by equipping you—the citizen—with thoroughly researched, unbiased, and easily digestible information. We believe an empowered citizenry is the cornerstone of a transparent and accountable local government. Yet, in an era where this should be the norm, both transparency and accountability often fall short. We're here to challenge this status quo. Our platform is designed to streamline and consolidate the essential information you need to become an informed voter, breaking down the barriers that often deter civic participation in a process that can and should be more straightforward."

Site Sections

  1. The Election: Navigate an interactive primary ballot that provides concise but comprehensive profiles for each candidate. We've distilled complex qualifications and policy positions into an easily understandable format.
  2. The Issues: Cut through the clutter by exploring major community issues presented in a straightforward manner, allowing you to quickly gauge where each candidate stands.
  3. Get Involved: Don't just be a spectator—be an actor in the democratic process. Our consolidated calendar guides you to meaningful opportunities for community and political engagement.
  4. Our Process: Understand the meticulous steps we've taken to bring you this resource. Our journey to advocate for improved community engagement, transparency, and accountability has been long but is a shared responsibility—one that should be easier to undertake, but often isn't.

By transforming intricate political landscapes into accessible insights, we aim to turn passive citizens into proactive community members. Your vote counts, your voice matters, and together, we can redefine what it means to be engaged, transparent, and accountable in our local politics.

This version attempts to combine the emphasis on individual responsibility with the work you've put into making complex information accessible. It also nods to the systemic issues that make this work necessary in the first place. Feel free to tweak further as needed!
