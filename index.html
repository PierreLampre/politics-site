Final report Election Season 2024

Read to get an idea of the upcoming elections here in Cumberland County.

US Senate - Curtis Bashaw is not your stereotypical Republican and Andy Kim is very party line. If you are a moderate or independent, Bashaw is definitely worth your consideration. Mooneyham and Khalil also had well laid out agendas, they probably deserved a spot on the debate stage.

US House of Representatives - Whether you like Van Drew or not, he's a powerhouse. With Salerno having a questionable campaign, he might not be currently strong enough to beat his opponent.

Cumberland County Commissioners - If it ain't broke, don't fix it. Cumberland County Democrats fail to make a compelling case for change, at least from where I'm sitting.

Cumberland County Clerk - Both candidates seem qualified and Riley is the incumbent, but a few potential mistakes during election season may have left her vulnerable to her challenger, Commissioner Lods.

Millville City Commissioner - All candidates seem like good choices, but Ranello is the incumbent and last minute issues may not be enough to compel a change.

Lawrence Township Committee - Incumbent Miletta has 17 years of experience compared to his newcomer challenger Woody, but Woody makes a compelling case for change.

Vineland City Council and Mayor races - Team Fanucci is a powerhouse with no visible case for change from challenger Arroyo on the council, and a campaign mostly based on attacks from former Mayor Romano.

Bridgeton, Fairfield, and Lawrence School boards - Extreme lack of information about these races. We need better press coverage.

Commercial Township School Board - 3 seats are open with 4 candidates. The three who communicated seem like good choices, with the only candidate Tracy Richardson being unknown from our perspective.

Vineland School Board - There are lots of ways to look at this, and we wish all candidates had fully communicated, possibly with a candidates forum to dive in deep.

VOTE WITH CONFIDENCE

At Inform the Vote, our mission is to establish a new standard of civic engagement by equipping you—the citizen—with thoroughly researched, unbiased, and easily digestible information.

We believe an empowered citizenry is the cornerstone of a transparent and accountable local government. Yet, in an era where this should be the norm, both transparency and accountability often fall short. We're here to challenge this status quo.

Our platform is designed to streamline and consolidate the essential information you need to become an informed voter, breaking down the barriers that often deter civic participation in a process that can and should be more straightforward.
