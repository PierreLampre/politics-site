US Senate Issues

An organized list of issues of contention this election cycle.

National Issues

Inflation
Inflation remains a significant economic challenge, impacting various aspects of life from the cost of goods to the stability of financial markets. While wage increases provide some relief, they have not kept up with the rapid rise in living costs, leading to decreased purchasing power for many Americans. What plans do the candidates have for ensuring US citizens can prosper in the near future?
Foreign Affairs
Conflicts around the world continue with situations like the conflict in Ukraine-Russia, the war with Israel-Hamas, increasing tensions between Taiwan-China, and more. How will the candidates handle these conflicts in a way that will keep us safe?
Border Security
The southern border has seen a record number of migrants crossing over during the past several years. How will the candidates address the amount of people coming to the border and those that have already crossed?
Health Care Affordability
The cost of healthcare remains a top issue, particularly for Democrats. Many Americans are concerned about the high costs of medical care and the need for more affordable health insurance options.
Partisan Cooperation
There is significant concern about the ability of Republicans and Democrats to work together. This issue is seen as critical across the political spectrum, reflecting a desire for more bipartisan efforts to address national problems.
