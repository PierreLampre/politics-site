quick summary

County Commissioner

This year the County Clerk seat is open for election. The current Democrat Clerk, Celeste Riley, is running for re-election against Republican challenger, Commissioner Lods. Both parties appear to be running 'slate', which means they are running as a team within their parties, potentially all the way up to the national level. All candidates have been reached out to in terms of clarifying their individual characteristics, agendas, and comparisons to other candidates. None have responded. So, if you vote party line, as of now, that's fair. If you're more of an independent who votes for individuals as opposed to parties, their is no significant information as of yet.

Celeste Riley, the incumbent, inherently has experience, so it is the opinion of Inform the Vote that there ought to be some kind of discussion as to why the Republican challenger would be significantly better or different than the incumbent, especially in terms of the specific local role they will hold. It's difficult to assess how candidates will perform differently from each other with only similar bios among candidates and a 'party-line' approach when many party-line talking points might not directly apply at the local level or to the role of County Clerk.
