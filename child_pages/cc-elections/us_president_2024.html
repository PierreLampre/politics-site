US President

This year the US Presidential seat is open for re-election. After concerns about President Biden's mental decline were highlighted at the debate, the Democrat party has switched in Vice President Kamala Harris as the new nominee, and she has recently picked Minnesota Governor Tim Walz as her running mate. Former president Donald J. Trump is the nominee for the Republicans, and recent events have given him some points in the polls. Despite not being included in the recent CNN debate, we are aware that Robert F. Kennedy Jr. is also on the NJ ballot. This will probably continue to be a wild race.

ELECTION DATE: November 7th, 2023
