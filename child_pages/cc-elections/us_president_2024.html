US President

This year the US Presidential seat is open for re-election. The incumbent, Joe Biden has just dropped out of the race and we are unsure of what that means moving forward. Former president Donald J. Trump is the nominee for the Republicans, and recent events have given him some points in the polls. Despite not being included in the recent CNN debate, we are aware that Robert F. Kennedy Jr. is also on the NJ ballot. This will probably continue to be a wild race.

ELECTION DATE: November 7th, 2023
