Republican Primary for US Senate

This year 1 seat is open in New Jersey for the US Senate, where incumbent Bob Menendez is likely to face serious challenges considering a recent scandal regarding alleged corruption. Four or five candidates are running for the Republican nomination, as Peter Vallorosi may or may not be switching to an indepedent.
Curtis Bashaw, a successful businessman who has various political leadership experience, is the Cumberland County Republican favorite as Mike Testa is his campaign chair.
Christine Serrano-Glassner is the current mayor of Mendham, NJ and she seems to have a good balance of political and business experience.
Albert Harshaw is a highly decorated military vet who was an international law major and is certified in maritime law.
Justin Murphy is a big advocate for military funding as he wants to be able to fund two wars simultaneously, secure the border with military resources, and increase military presence in the South China Sea.
Peter Vallorosi is very focused on a pure application of the constitution and high transparency of the government on a single webiste.
