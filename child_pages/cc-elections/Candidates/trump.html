Donald J. Trump

As the main focus of this site is on local politics and there is plenty of coverage on the presidential race, we recommend that you follow it yourself. Partial Voting record

  • Tax Cuts and Jobs Act of 2017: This was a significant piece of legislation that Trump signed into law, which lowered the corporate tax rate and aimed to stimulate economic growth. It was a key promise of his campaign.
  • Bipartisan Budget Act of 2019: Trump signed this bill, which increased federal spending and raised the debt ceiling, reflecting his administration's willingness to expand fiscal policy despite rising national debt. Politifact​
  • Trump made multiple attempts to repeal the Affordable Care Act (Obamacare). Although a full repeal was unsuccessful, his administration rolled back certain provisions, including the individual mandate that required Americans to have health insurance​ VoteSmart
  • USMCA Trade Agreement: Trump fulfilled a campaign promise by renegotiating NAFTA, resulting in the United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement (USMCA), which altered trade rules in several sectors, including automotive and agriculture​
  • Vetoes on Military and Security Issues: Trump vetoed several resolutions that sought to limit his administration’s military actions, including arms sales to Saudi Arabia and the U.S. involvement in Yemen​
  • First Step Act of 2018: Trump signed this bipartisan criminal justice reform bill, which aimed to reduce recidivism and reform sentencing laws, particularly for non-violent offenses​
