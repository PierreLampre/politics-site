Robert McQuade

Robert Mcquade has political experience from previously sitting on the Millville Board of Education for 9 years, he was a Millville City Commissioner for about a year, and served on the Cumberland County Prosecutor's Office law enforcement advisory board. He co-founded the Center City Crime Watch and was recognized by the Millville Commissioners board for helping police apprehend a subject Daily Journal article. However, it seems as though he was arrested for a similar incident, though we're not sure anything came of it as it appears as though the matter was settled in court and we have no information as to whether it affected his role on the school board... and his involvement may have been related to his prior experience as a 'Special' police officer and co-founder of Center City Crime Watch. NJ dot com article. The following is paraphrased from communication with McQuade, and we hope to get more information soon:
