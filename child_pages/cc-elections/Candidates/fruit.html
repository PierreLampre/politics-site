Rachele Fruit

Rachele Fruit has been running for office in various places since the 70's. She is a Jewish member of the Socialist Worker's Party who was active in the fight against apartheid. She is concerned about the rise of anti-semitism since the attack on October 7th, and she supports Israel's right to exist while understanding the Palestinian struggle... as in, the problem is with Hamas, not Palestinians. She supports unions and believes that third parties need to exist in the political arena to support the rights of workers as opposed to the ruling class.
