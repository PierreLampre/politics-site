quick summary

Robert A. Austino

Austino has lengthy experience working in government as sheriff, but no direct experience as a commissioner. As per a campaign picture on his facebook campaign site, his agenda seems to be to improve roads/infrastructure/broadband, support senior housing and healthcare, and provide recreational advancements.

When asked at Deerfield Day to clarify his agenda, policy positions, and comparing that to the incumbents he is challenging, Austino basically said (paraphrased): "My big thing is money and budget. The county is attempting to build 3 county parks when our budget is stretched. I helped run a budget for 15 years, so I will help bring fiscal responsibility to the board of commissioners." Be sure to check our county issues page to get a more detailed look at what's going on in the county. Also, Austino and Capizola have openly supported the Harris/Walz ticket on facebook, so it follows that Austino/Capizola will support and/or implement that agenda, unless they clarify otherwise in the future.
