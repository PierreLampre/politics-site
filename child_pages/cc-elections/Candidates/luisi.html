Luisi wants to provide in person meetings again, and expand the Surrogate’s Office to accommodate the different schedules of residents. Skip understands what families are experiencing during an emotional and often difficult time from the loss of a loved one. EXPERIENCE

In addition to being the owner and manager of the Demarco-Luisi Funeral Home in Vineland for 35 years, Skip has been a licensed funeral director for 50 years. He was involved in the West Jersey Funeral Director Association, his local and civic memberships include being the Founder and Past President of the Italian American Benevolent Association, a member of the American Legion Post 0270, he is the past member of the Vineland Housing Authority and was a member on the Vineland Zoning Board. Lifelong resident of VIneland
