Claudia De La Cruz

Ms. De La Cruz has been politically active for the past 30 years, involving herself in many ways from being a church pastor, community organizer, participating in movements regarding Puerto Rico, Haiti, Palestine, and more. She has a socialist platform where she wants to 'end capitalism' and 'seize the biggest 100 corporations', providing guaranteed social programs by making them constitutional rights, imposing a 100% tax on income over ten million dollars, etc. She aims to take money out of politics (like corporate lobbying), wants to end aid to Israel to the extent that she has participated in the recent protests, wants to cut the military budget by 90%, wants to war on black America (reparations, increase affirmative action, address police brutality, etc.), unrestricted and free abortion access, full equality for the LGBTQ community, and immediate takeover of the fossil fuel industry. As a reminder, our focus for this site is local politics, and this is only a brief summary.
