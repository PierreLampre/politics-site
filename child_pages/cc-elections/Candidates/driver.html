quick summary

Jessica N. Driver

Candidate Driver has been a Commercial Township School Board member for the past 3 and a half years with other relevant credentials such as having kids in the school system, being an active parent and volunteer in the school system, years of teaching experience as a special education paraeducator, served on various school committees and sometimes being committee chair, and PTA secretary for a few years. Her main agenda items are to provide a safe environment for all school children and to provide transparency throughout the school system for the community. She voted to increase school bus driver pay and substitute pay, and voted yes for the sale of the PNS system. We are planning an interview with candidate Driver to help clarify some of the information she provided and to dive deeper into some of the mentioned issues.
