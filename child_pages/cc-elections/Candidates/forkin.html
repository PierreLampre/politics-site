Thomas J. Forkin

Mr. Forkin has a well rounded resume including experience in politics and business, and he is Republican/supports conservative values. While directly communicating with him we have learned that his specific agenda relates to establishing prudent line item spending with the school budget, scaffolding continuing educational development, and continuing to educate the other board members on how to save resources for the taxpayers in relation to legal issues. He also referred us to his facebook page, where we can also see some things that might be related, including opposing Drag Queen Reading Hour, opposing DEI, a general disliking of the Department of Education, preventing cell phone use in classrooms, and supporting fairness in women's sports.
