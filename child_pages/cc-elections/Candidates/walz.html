Tim Walz

Minnesota Governor Tim Walz was a member of the national guard for nearly 25 years, was a congressman for over a decade, and has been governor for nearly five years. During that time he has supported the Affordable Care Act, improved Veterans Affairs, voted for the National Defense Authorization Act, supported farmers in rural Minnesota, promoted funding for public schools and making college more affordable, and generally tried to reduce carbon emissions and promoted renewable energy. He seems to have a moderate stance on gun control, being a gun owner himself while pushing common sense gun law legislation. While he does have a lot of experience and has made many accomplishments, it is still unclear what the specific Harris-Walz agenda will be, since as of today 8/9/2024 no agenda is posted on their campaign site. One might be able to speculate based on the Democrat Party Platform, but we here at InformTheVote don't want to speculate too much. Hopefully as the campaign season evolves his platform will become clearer. Click the red x at the top of this box and then scroll down down for more in-depth information and sources.

experience

  • Army National Guard for 24 years.
  • High School teacher and football coach.
  • Minnesota Congressman for 12 years., 2007-2019
  • Minnesota governor for 5 years, 2019-present.
  • Ranking member of the House Veterans Affairs Committee.


Partial Voting record

  • Supported the Affordable Care Act in 2010 and voted against multiple repeals.
  • Supported numerous bills to improve veterans' healthcare, benefits, and services.​​
  • Voted for the National Defense Authorization Act​
  • Supported measures to provide better equipment and support for troops.​
  • Voted for the Farm Bill, which provided subsidies and support for the agricultural sector.​
  • Voted to increase funding for public schools and making college more affordable.
  • Voted generally in favor of reducing carbon emissions and promoting renewable energy sources.
