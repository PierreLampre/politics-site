individual agenda

Quote taken directly from communication with Figueroa:

My biggest purpose of being on the board is to make sure the district meets the needs of its growing and changing population, especially I regards to diversity. My goal is to meet these needs and uphold the history of respect and high standards of service this school has always provided.
