quick summary

Donna M. Perez

Donna Perez has about 35 years of experiene in the financial industry as a financial advisor. She has also served on many boards throughout the years including the Vineland Chamber of Commerce, the Cumberland County Board of Trustees, and at Inspira. She hopes to bring her expertise to the Vineland School Board with a common sense approach to help balance the policies of the state with the specific needs of our schools here in Southern New Jersey. With this approach she hopes to help teachers focus on teaching, serve the needs of the students so they can excel, and keep parents notified as to what is going on. individual agenda

Direct quote from communication with Perez:

"I don't have a specific agenda. However, I believe my past experience as well as my financial industry experience would be an asset to the board. I do feel the BOE's role is to provide our staff with as much support as possible. Allowing our teaching professionals to "teach" and not get bogged down with issues that go beyond educating our students. Also, I believe we need to provide our student population with the best education possible. Using our tax dollars to provide an education that will prepare them for higher education, heading into the workforce or military, is our most important mandate."


Stance on Issues

Direct quote from communication with Perez:

"My stance on issues such as fiscal responsibility, parental notification, higher test scores, school safety is to take a common sense approach. Meaning, while I understand there are mandates from the State that must be adhered to, we need to look at those policy's that the district can or cannot implement and make changes accordingly. Vineland School District's needs may differ from the needs of students in North Jersey, therefore, there is not a "one size fits all" when it comes to making policy decisions for our student population."
