quick summary

Curtis Bashaw

Candidate Bashaw is a self-described 'outsider' running in the Republican primary for US Senate. While he says he is an outsider, he does have a lot of well-documented leadership awards for serving in politically related roles. He also has a lot of experience as a successful businessman in real estate and hospitality. He seems to have decided to jump directly into politics in response to the government's handling of COVID and how it affected small businesses. He wants to secure the border while revamping legal immigration policy to encourage legal immigration. While Inform the Vote has only briefly spoken to Bashaw, he has been hitting the campaign trail hard, which is well documented on Twitter/X. While there is a lot of information below regarding his agenda, be sure to go over the Republican Party Platform and Agenda 47 (Donald Trump's agenda) to get a broader idea of how he might handle the position. experience

While Mr. Bashaw may not have been directly involved in up to this point, he has been around politics his whole life. His grandfather had a political radio show that the government shut down, and so his grandfather rented a boat to take his show off the coast and out of the jurisdiction so he could continue to broadcast. His grandfather also had a strong conviction that political discussions ought to be based in listening and persuasion rather than telling people exactly what to think or censoring unfavorable views.
