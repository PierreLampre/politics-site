The quick summary for Candidate Bashaw: Candidate Bashaw is a self-described 'outsider' running in the Republican primary for US Senate. While he says he is an outsider, he does have a lot of well-documented leadership awards for serving in political related roles. He also has a lot of experience as a successful businessman in real estate and hospitality. He seems to have decided to jump directly into politics in response to the government's handling of COVID and how it affected small businesses. He wants to secure the border while revamping legal immigration policy to encourage legal immigration. While Inform the Vote has only briefly spoken to Bashaw, he has been hitting the campaign trail hard, which is well documented on Twitter/X. Scroll down for more in-depth information and sources.
