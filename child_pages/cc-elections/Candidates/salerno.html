Joe Salerno

Candidate Salerno is a successful businessman with degrees in engineering and law. One of his main campaign messages seems to be a response to Congressman Van Drew appearing to be a sell-out to former President Trump and other politicians in Washington, instead of keeping the constituents of South Jersey as the top priority. Scrolling through his Facebook page shows many direct attack ads in that vein. He has a bunch of agenda items such as protecting reproductive rights, instituting tax credits to bring manufacturing back, protecting social security, and more.
