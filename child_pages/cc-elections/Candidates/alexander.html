quick summary

Tim Alexander

Candidate Alexander is a former detective captain and major trials prosecutor in Philadelphia who was inspired to join the force because he was racially profiled in his youth, so he wanted to bring systemic change to the force. He has many left leaning items on his agenda including being pro-choice, addressing the gender wage gap, passing the Equality Act, supporting climate change policy, and more.

The Difference
When asked how he would differentiate himself from the other candidates at a Democrat meet and greet event, candidate Alexander pointed out that he knows the district since he's lived here his whole life, while others have not. He also knows that this is not a progressive district and he is not running a progressive campaign. He also recognizes that the country is in a deep debt and therefore he took the time to make sure all of his proposals have a proper budget that won't put us further into debt.

experience

Candidate Alexander is a former Detective Captain. He was inspired to be a police officer because he was racially profiled. He has been a systemic racism fighter. He was a major Trials Prosecutor for Philadelphia District Attorney’s Office. He was a member of the Philadelphia Youth Aid Panel. He is now “Of Counsel” for Helmer, Conley, and Kasselman. He is a civil rights advocate. He was a business Management major from Drexel University. He joined the Civil Rights Unit for the Philadelphia Solicitor’s Office. He concentrates on Civil Rights and Employment Discrimination in his private practice.
