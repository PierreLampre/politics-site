quick summary

Celeste Riley

Current County Clerk Celeste Riley is running for re-election this year. She is currently serving her second term, which means she inherently has more experience in this position than her Republican challenger, Commissioner Lods. She was an Assemblywoman in the 3rd district, having won re-election for that position, and served several committee positions during her tenure in that position. She has served as both a Bridgeton City Council member and the Bridgeton City Council president. A facebook post on August 13th states that her agenda will include streamlining county services, implementing transparency and accountability, and implementing community engagement. We have reached out to her for clarifications on overall agenda, policy, and comparisons to her challenger, but we are still working out whether she will provide information directly to us for concerns regarding potential partisanship on our part. During the process of trying to contact candidates for election related information, we reached out to her offices several times and were able to get useful contact information. As of now 9/1/2024, there have been some issues regarding the Clerk's office regarding things such as the design of the ballot and how office literature is being presented to the public. You can also view what looks like a video of her and John Capizola here, and here, but we haven't had time to review them yet. It also seems as though they have been posting more regular information in their faebook group, so be sure to follow that if you'd like to stay current. Similarly, the Cumberland NJ Dems have been regularly posting as well. Click the x at the top red of this box to go back to the page.
