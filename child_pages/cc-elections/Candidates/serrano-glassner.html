Christine Serrano-Glassner

Candidate Serrano-Glassner is the current mayor of Mendham and she seems to have a very good balance of business and political experience. She spent many years in the culinary field and then was a Director of Operations in the political field, then a regional advocate for small business in the US government, then 7 years with the Empire State Development Corporation, then 2 years as a member of city council, and then 6 years as mayor. She wants to tackle many issues such as reducing inflation, protecting social security, and medicare, increase funding for law enforcement to reduce crime, and more.
