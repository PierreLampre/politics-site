Robert Romano
Former mayor Robert Romano has entered the race for mayor again. After searching around on the internet for a bit, there seems to be very little recent information about him, except for an alleged drunk driving incident back in March Courier Post Article. A prior race in 2016 had his agenda focused on things such as improving the attractions on Landis Avenue and cutting waste from city taxes, Daily Journal Article, but we're not sure how relevant that is to 2024. We made contact with Romano via facebook, but he was hesitant about making his comments public.
