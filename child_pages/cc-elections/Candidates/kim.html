Andy Kim

Andy Kim is a current Congressman who was a Rhoads Scholar, a national security expert at the Pentagon, and a civilan advisor to the military in Afghanistan. He has a mix of left and right leaning agenda items such as supporting universal healthcare and supporting pro-choice policies, while also supporting small businesses and reducing red tape.
