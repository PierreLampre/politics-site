POLICY POSITIONS

  1. Helped 4,000 residents receive unemployment.

  4. Represented small businesses as a lawyer to help them re-open during the pandemic
  5. Opposed Murphy borrowing $9.9 billion to balance the state budget. Through Testa’s lawsuit, the borrowing was reduced to $4.5 billion because the borrowing reduced its scope to funds lost specifically due to the pandemic.
