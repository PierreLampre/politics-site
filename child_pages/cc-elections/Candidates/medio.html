quick summary

Eugene Medio
Eugene Medio has many years of experience on the Vineland School Board and is running for re-election this year. Some things to note are that he spoke out against policy 5756 ('Parents rights vs Trans rights' issue), he wants a general overhaul of disciplinary policy, and believes the superintendent should be replaced. We have been communicating with Medio and expect more information regarding his agenda, policy positions, and comparisons to the other candidates. individual agenda

Opposes the 'credible messenger' approach to dealing with troubled youth. Credible Messenger Website

He is for parental rights, against transgender normalization, and against new style sex ed in schools. He voted to repeal policy 5756 this past year while serving on the board and made comments about his decision. Local Lens Article

He wants to focus on literacy in schools.

He is not a fan of the superintendent and believes he needs to be replaced.

He opposes restorative practices in terms of handling discipline in schools because he believes it gives trouble-making students too many chances leaving schools and the teaching process to be disrupted too regularly. Article Explaining Restorative Practices

experience

He is a Georgetown University graduate. He has many years of experience as a school board member.
