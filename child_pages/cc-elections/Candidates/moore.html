While currently serving in local government, Moore has not established a campaign website or provided public documentation of his platform. Agenda

Lawrence Moore has not published a formal campaign website or detailed policy platform. However, based on public statements and his professional background, Moore emphasizes affordability, public safety, and efficient governance. He advocates for reducing wasteful government spending, cutting taxes, and reforming school funding to ensure equitable education opportunities. Moore also supports the use of nuclear energy as a cost-effective solution for the state's energy needs. While these positions provide a glimpse into his priorities, a comprehensive campaign platform would offer voters a more detailed understanding of his legislative objectives.​

Background and Campaign Message

Lawrence Moore serves as the Deputy Mayor of Harrison Township, where he leads initiatives focused on economic development, infrastructure improvement, and sustainable growth. He also holds the position of Deputy Municipal Clerk for South Harrison Township, managing planning and zoning, tax collection, vital records, and administrative operations. Moore's experience includes serving as a legislative aide for former New Jersey Assemblywoman Beth Sawyer, providing him with insight into policy development and constituent services. Academically, he holds a bachelor's degree in political science from Rutgers-Camden University and is pursuing a master's in public administration at the University of Pennsylvania's Fels Institute of Government. With over 12 years of service as a volunteer firefighter and as captain of the Harmony Fire Company, Moore demonstrates a strong commitment to public safety and community engagement.
