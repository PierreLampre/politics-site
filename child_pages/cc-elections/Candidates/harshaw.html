quick summary

Albert Harshaw

Albert Harshaw is a Republican candidate for US Senate. He is a highly decorated military vet with who was an international business law major and is certified in maritime law. He has very detailed information on his site regarding not only his specific policy ideas, but also how he plans on achieving those policy ideas. His policy proposals range from rebuilding manufacturing, agriculture, and overall infrastructure, to investing in childhood education, re-entering the space race, bringing accountability to government corruption, reforming the tax code, and more. Harshaw was also very prompt and thorough while communicating with Inform the Vote. During a phone interview, Harshaw differentiated himself from other candidates by saying he has been developing his policies for years while other candidates are 'Johnny come latelys'. Phone call interview



Albert Harshaw explained that he has spent years developing his policies while others are 'Johnny come latelys' He believes in staying open-minded when negotiating policy and that the best idea should win, which he learned through his experience in the military. He emphasized the need to bring back energy independence. He also mentioned that he helped the battle against the recent county line issue regarding voting, which had elements of implying favoritism for candidates who were on 'the county line'. He is also very open to coming to Cumberland County to campaign at local events.



experience

Highly decorated navy veteran who served in Iraq and Quwait. He received a NATO medal for serving during Bosnia. He has experience with fiscal responsibility as he is a small business owner who has managed several restaurants and worked for Kawasaki Rail Car. His was an international law major and he is certified in maritime law. His family has a long tradition of community service and service to the country.
