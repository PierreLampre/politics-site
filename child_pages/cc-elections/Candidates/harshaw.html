The quick summary for Candidate Harshaw: Albert Harshaw is a Republican candidate for US Senate. He is a highly decorated military vet with who was an international business law major and is certified in maritime law. He has very detailed information on his site regarding not only his specific policy ideas, but also how he plans on achieving those policy ideas. His policy proposals range from rebuilding manufacturing, agriculture, and overall infrastructure, to investing in childhood education, re-entering the space race, bringing accountability to government corruption, reforming the tax code, and more.
