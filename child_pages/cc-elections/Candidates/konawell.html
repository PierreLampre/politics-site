Chris Konawell

Chris Konawell is a Gloucester County Commissioner currently serving his first term on the board, where he oversees the Department of Government Services. A small business owner and entrepreneur, Konawel highlights his private sector experience as a foundation for supporting job creation and economic growth. Agenda

Chris Konawel has not released a formal campaign platform or public policy agenda for his Assembly bid. However, his tenure as a Gloucester County Commissioner and his professional background suggest a focus on economic development, business-friendly policies, and efficient government operations. As a small business owner, Konawel emphasizes job creation and economic growth, aiming to foster a business-friendly environment in Gloucester County. His involvement with organizations such as the New Jersey Independent Auto Dealers Association and the South Jersey Real Estate Investors Association further indicates his commitment to supporting local businesses and economic initiatives. While these aspects provide some insight into his priorities, a comprehensive campaign platform would offer voters a clearer understanding of his legislative goals.

Background and Experience

Chris Konawel is currently serving his first term as a Gloucester County Commissioner, where he oversees the Department of Government Services, including the Construction Board of Appeals, Medical Examiner, Surrogate Court, and County Clerk. An entrepreneur and small business owner, Konawel has created jobs and managed multimillion-dollar growth in his ventures. He is actively involved in various organizations, including the New Jersey Independent Auto Dealers Association, the MidAtlantic Region Independent Auto Dealers Association, the National Independent Auto Dealers Association, and the South Jersey Real Estate Investors Association. A resident of Glassboro, Konawel is also a Jiu-Jitsu enthusiast, having won championships in IBJJF and NAGA competitions. His diverse experiences in business and public service contribute to his perspective on economic and governmental matters.
