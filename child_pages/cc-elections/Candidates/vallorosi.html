quick summary

Peter Vallorosi

Peter Vallorosi is an independent candidate for US Senate. He seems to be very focused on a very pure application of the constitution and applying full transparency of government business through a regularly updated, single source website. He enumerates many ideas throughout his website, including simplifying the tax code, balancing the budget, reducing the size of government, removing donor influence from politics, and much more. Vallorosi was also very prompt with communicating with Inform the Vote. Click the red x at the top of this box and then scroll down down for more in-depth information and sources.
