Chase Oliver

Chase Oliver is the presidential nominee for the Libertarian party. The platform on his site lays out some interesting policy positions regarding reducing our military presence around the world, implementing nuclear energy to gain energy independence, imposing term limits on capitol hill, and more. However, many of his agenda items are briefly explained without a clear vision on how they will be implemented. As this site is more dedicated to local politics, we will leave it up to you to dive deeper if you're interested in him, by exploring his website and socials.
