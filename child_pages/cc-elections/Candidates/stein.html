Jill Stein

Jill Stein is the Green Party's presidential nominee. Her agenda includes introducing an economic bill of rights that guarantees lifelong free public education, guaranteed free childcare, removing loopholes for wealth taxes, and more. For labor, she wants to pass a $25 minimum wage, guarantee housing as a human right, close the pay gap and end other discriminatory practices, and more. In fact, she does have quite a lengthy list of agenda items on her site, but not so much detail on how the implementation would work. Again, as this site is more focused on local politics, if you're interested in Jill Stein as a candidate, we encourage you to follow her and look into her platform for yourself. You can also see her debate presidential candidates Chase Oliver and Randall Terry here. Click the red x at the top of this box and then scroll down down for more in-depth information and sources.
