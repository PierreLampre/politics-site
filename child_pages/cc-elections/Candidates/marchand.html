Candidates Forum Statements

When asked at the Candidates Forum at Bridgeton High School what makes him the most qualified for the position of Commissioner, Marchand spoke about his experience as an attorney in Bridgeton where he owns his own practice, his experience on the school board, and his passionate desire to make the County a safe and attractive place for his family to thrive.
