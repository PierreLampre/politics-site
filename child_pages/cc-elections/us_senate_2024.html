US Senate NJ

This year 1 seat is open for election in the US Senate in NJ. The incumbent, Bob Menendez is running as an independent in general response to his recent convictions. The Republicans have nominated Curtis Bashaw in a somewhat unexpected victory over Trump endorsed candidate Serrano-Glassner. The Democrats nominated current Congressman Andy Kim. This looks like it will be quite an interesting race, as Menendez's conviction will probably affect his campaign significantly, Andy Kim appears to be a somewhat moderate Democrat, and Bashaw is backed by our local Republicans who seem to have a winning strategy over the past several years. We are also aware of candidate Peter Vallorosi who will be running as a more straightforward independent, who may shake things up with his pure constitutionalist perspectives.

ELECTION DATE: November 7th, 2023
