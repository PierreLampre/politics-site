Democrat Primary for US President

As you probably know, President Biden is running for re-election. What you might not realize is that he is technically involved in the New Jersey primary race against Terrisa Bukovinac. Bukovinac, a self-called 'pro-life progressive', admits that she will lose the race against Biden, but she is using it as a platform to raise awareness of her pro-life platform.
