US Senate Issues

An organized list of issues of contention this election cycle.

2nd District Related Issues

Economy and Jobs
Economic growth and job creation are vital for New Jersey’s prosperity, especially in the wake of economic challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic. How will the candidates ensure us that we will continue to recover fiscally in the coming years?
Healthcare Access
Access to affordable healthcare for all residents, tackling the opioid crisis, and addressing public health emergencies have been in focus in New Jersey. How will the candidates address such issues?
Infrastructure
Modernizing infrastructure to support economic growth, improve transportation, and enhance quality of life will help areas like Cumberland County become more competitive in the business sector. How will the candidates improve infrastructure moving forward?
Education
Investing in education to ensure that all New Jersey residents have access to high-quality learning opportunities from early childhood through higher education. How will the candidates ensure that our children are properly prepared for the future?
Environment and Climate Change
Addressing climate change and protecting New Jersey’s natural resources are critical for the state’s environmental and economic future. What are the candidates' plans for handling the environment?
Social Justice and Equality
Promoting social justice and equality to ensure that all residents have the opportunity to succeed and thrive could unite the country. How will the candidates address people from all backgrounds needs?
Public Safety and Gun Control
Ensuring public safety while respecting constitutional rights is a key concern for New Jersey residents. What vision do the candidates have for striking a proper balance between protecting 2nd amendment rights, common sense gun control, and appropriate policing practices?
Immigration
Immigration remains a contentious issue throughout the country and New Jersey is no exception. How will the candidates ensure we have a secure border without impeding on good-faith, legal immigration?
Housing and Urban Development
Homelessness seems to be on the rise throughout the country. How will the candidates ensure that our citizens will be looked after in an affordable manner?
Veterans’ Affairs
Supporting veterans and their families is a priority, ensuring they have access to the benefits and services they deserve. How will the candidates ensure the people who dedicated their lives to protecting us are taken care of?
