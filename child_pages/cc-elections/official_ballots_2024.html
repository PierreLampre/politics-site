Official Ballots Posting Explanation

Given that we are not trying to mislead anyone and the recent situation regarding the general election ballot design, we have made this page to ensure you have a resource to study the final ballot design when it becomes available. We have an OPRA request with the clerk's office about this and hope to receive a response soon. If we have time, we will redesign our overall website to more accurately reflect that official ballot, but being that we have limited time before early voting and need to stay current with what's actually happening, posting links to files or posting pictures of the ballot will have to do for now, rather than spend hours redesigning and reprogramming the site. Click the red x at the top of this box and then scroll down down for more in-depth information and sources.
